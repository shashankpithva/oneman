Set up real email sending

    
Polsia sends real email through your own free EmailJS account. Create one at emailjs.com, connect an email service, build a template, then paste the three IDs below. Stored only in this browser.

    

    

    

    

    
    

    
Important — so Polsia’s email is what actually sends (not the template’s own text):
In your EmailJS template, delete any sample/hard-coded copy and set the fields to these EXACT variables:
• Subject = {{subject}}
• Content / body = {{message}} — switch the content box to the code/HTML view and paste only this, so it is not escaped
• To Email = {{to_email}}
• From Name = {{from_name}}   Reply To = {{reply_to}}
If the body contains any fixed text instead of {{message}}, recipients get the template, not Polsia’s email.

  





  

    
    
Posting to your socials

    
No setup, no webhook, no fees. When you approve a social post in the Outbox, Polsia opens the network's composer with your text already filled in — you just hit Post to confirm.

    

      𝕏 / X — opens the post box pre-filled. One click to publish.

      LinkedIn — opens the share composer pre-filled. One click to publish.

      Instagram — copies your caption and opens Instagram (their rules don't allow pre-filling), so you just paste it in.
    

    
Why not fully hands-off? Auto-posting safely needs your private login tokens on a server, and this site is public — any key placed here could be stolen and used to post as you. The 1-click composer keeps your accounts safe and stays completely free.
