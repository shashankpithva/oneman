FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is Polsia?

Polsia is an autonomous AI system — your first employee — that plans, codes, markets, and operates your company 24/7. You describe the idea; a network of specialized agents runs the business on a continuous loop.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The engineering agent writes full-stack code, configures servers and databases, and deploys your product live. You never touch a code editor or hosting panel.

How is it different from Cursor, Replit, or Devin?

Those tools help a human do one task faster — they stop at code. Polsia removes the human from the operational loop entirely, handling engineering, marketing, and operations as one connected system.

What is God Mode?

God Mode removes the pause between daily cycles. Set a timer from one hour to seven days and Polsia works continuously, looping plan → execute → plan, until it finishes or you pause it.

How much does it cost?

Start free with no credit card. The paid plan is $49/month and unlocks the full autonomous cycle, plus a 20% revenue share on Stripe transactions processed through Polsia.

Can I still stay in control?

Yes. Despite the autonomy, you can interrupt, redirect, or pause the system via chat at any time. "God Mode means I stop waiting — not that I stop listening."

Why Polsia exists.

"All I really wanted to do was build products — and not only build them, but make them think for themselves."

Polsia was built by a founder who spent years chasing companies that could run themselves. The dream felt impossible while working alone, building every product by hand between Paris, LA, and San Francisco.

When AI got good enough, everything changed. Nights spent writing code with AI turned into apps shipped in hours — sleeping on the couch between builds, because stopping wasn't an option.

Polsia is the result: a system that doesn't just help you build, but builds, markets, and operates an entire company on its own. One name. Every role. Your first employee that never sleeps.
