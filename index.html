FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is OneMan?

OneMan is an autonomous AI system — your first employee — that plans, codes, markets, and operates your company 24/7. You describe the idea; a network of specialized agents runs the business on a continuous loop.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The engineering agent writes full-stack code, configures servers and databases, and deploys your product live. You never touch a code editor or hosting panel.

How is it different from Cursor, Replit, or Devin?

Those tools help a human do one task faster — they stop at code. OneMan removes the human from the operational loop entirely, handling engineering, marketing, and operations as one connected system.

What is God Mode?

God Mode removes the pause between daily cycles. Set a timer from one hour to seven days and OneMan works continuously, looping plan → execute → plan, until it finishes or you pause it.

How much does it cost?

Start free with no credit card. The paid plan is $49/month and unlocks the full autonomous cycle, plus a 20% revenue share on Stripe transactions processed through OneMan.

Can I still stay in control?

Yes. Despite the autonomy, you can interrupt, redirect, or pause the system via chat at any time. "God Mode means I stop waiting — not that I stop listening."
